Frequently Asked Questions

What is Savvy?

Savvy enables users to search, verify, and share current prices for goods and services in a specific location. Savvy is for the consumer who wants to save their money in an easily accessible and quick to use application.

What are the benefits of signing up for Savvy?

Signing up for Savvy allows you to submit prices of products or services you've purchased. You can also keep track of prices you've submitted, and keep a list of products or services in order to track if their price changes.

How do I sign up for Savvy?

To sign up for Savvy, visit the Sign Up page.

How do I view prices for a product or service?

To view prices for any product or service, type the name of the product or service into the search bar at the top of every page.

What if there are no products or services found from my search?

If there are no results found, it means there is currently no submitted prices for that product or service. If you have purchased it, you can submit the price you paid for the product or service.

How do I submit a price for a product or service I bought?

Go to the Submit Price page and enter a name for the product or service, the place of purchase, the price you paid, and add any tags that you can think of which may help others search for it in the future. You can also upload a picture of the product or service.

Can I add more than one tag when submitting a price?

Yes. Simply enter another tag in the box and click add.

What if I forgot my password?

Don't panic! Just click here to reset your password. Enter the email address associated with your account and we will send you a password reset link.

What I lost access to the email account associated with my Savvy account?

Unfortunately at this time we can not modify the email address associated your Savvy account. However, you can always sign up for a new account here.
